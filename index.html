Proven Effective Method

Specializing in the humane removal of Canada geese and other nuisance birds

Wingin' It Goose Control provides services to Western Washington and the Seattle area. Our services include:

  • On-site removal - Patrolling for Canada geese and other wild birds using our highly trained goose dogs
  • Property consultations - Evaluating current and prospective Canada geese removal programs
  • The sale of trained dogs - For clients wishing to implement their own goose control programs
  • Goose dog training - For clients who currently own a working goose dog needing additional training

For help in deciding which of these strategies would best suit you or your company's needs, please read through the information on our site, or contact us to discuss any of these services. We would be happy to meet with you and explain all options for implementing an effective and long lasting goose control program
